How does this website work?

  • This website is similar to other wikis, but is focused on games
  • Anyone can write articles about their favorite game for anyone to see
  • The developers of this website will read the articles
  • At first, the article will be classified as an unofficial article until the developers review it
  • If they like the article, it will be categorized as an "official article" and will be formatted to appear better, and the article will be revised
  • Once the article has been converted to an “official article,” it cannot be revised further
  • If they believe the article still needs work, it will remain as an unofficial article, leaving the article open for other users to edit further and revise it
  • This system will motivate users to write high-quality articles and will ensure readers will know which articles are guaranteed to be high-quality

Who are the creators of this website?

  • There are 4 developers: Shivam, Ryan, Yoonki, and Ryu.
  • Shivam programmed the backend of the website
  • Ryan, Yoonki, and Ryu coded the HTML and the CSS of the website.
  • They also made the first “Official Articles” so other users have examples of how they should write their articles
